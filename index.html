Color Replacement Tool

This tool allows you to replace specific colors in PNG images with new colors. It uses advanced color matching algorithms to ensure smooth transitions and edge handling.

How to use: Upload a PNG image, click on a color you want to replace, select a new color, and click "Replace Color". You can save your favorite colors in the palette for quick access.
